Enter your birthday and we will tell you Is your birthday is a palindrome?

This app checks your birthday in 6 date formats like dd-mm-yyyy mm-dd-yyyy yyyy-mm-dd dd-mm-yy yy-mm-dd mm-dd-yy

e.g. if your birthday is 05 may 2001, then app will check for 24052001 ,05242001 ,20010524 ,240501 ,052401 ,010524
